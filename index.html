ABOUT ME

I'm a full-stack software engineer in Redlands, California with a customer-facing background in accounting and dispatching. I've always dreamed of coding ever since my younger years. It was one thing to be immersed in a video game, or interact with a website, but it was so much more interesting for me to think about how it all worked under the hood. Questions like "How am I able to 'drink' a potion, and have it 'disappear' from my inventory? Is it really gone?" or "Where does my user info live when I sign up? How does it remember me when I login?" lived rent-free in my mind.

Now that I have answers to these questions, looking back, I was just questioning concepts such as state & database management, authorization and authentication without realizing it! That curiosity birthed this motto of 'find it, & learn it' as an engine to my drive passion. So, since you've found me, if you're interested and want to learn more about my past experience, check out my resume!
